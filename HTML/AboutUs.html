Mission

GreenTech Solutions has set out on a big journy to try to make eco-frindly technology avaible to small buissnesses so that they can reduce their carbon footprint. We have also found a way to make it easiar to recycle for these buissnesses so that they can focus on what they do.

We aim to educate as well as offer practical soultions to many problems a buissness would face trying to go green.

Timeline of our Achievements

We aim to convert most of our partners to a eco frindly companies within 10-15 years of time. If that is to long we also offer other plans to convert faster than that. WE have converted over 5 companies in the las 10 years to all or molstly eco friendly alternitives.
